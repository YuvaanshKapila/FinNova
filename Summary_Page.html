How It Works: Live Crisis Scenarios

1

8 Crisis Scenarios

Teams are assigned to one of 8 unique financial crisis scenarios at the start of the hackathon. Each scenario presents different challenges: market crashes, banking system failures, cryptocurrency collapses, payment fraud attacks, and more.

2

Real-Time Updates

Throughout the 48 hours, crisis conditions evolve dynamically. Our team pushes live updates simulating breaking news, regulatory changes, market shifts, and emergency situations - forcing teams to adapt their solutions on the fly.

3

Bonus Points System

Teams can earn bonus points by successfully responding to crisis updates. Quick adaptation, innovative pivots, and resilient solutions that handle changing conditions earn additional scoring opportunities.

4

Build Under Pressure

Just like real fintech professionals, you'll need to build robust, scalable solutions while conditions change around you. This prepares you for the dynamic nature of the financial technology industry.

Traditional hackathons give you a static problem - we give you a living, breathing crisis that tests your ability to innovate under pressure. This is what makes Finnova Hacks the most realistic fintech hackathon experience in the world.
